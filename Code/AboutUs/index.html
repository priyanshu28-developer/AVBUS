ABOUT US

Aadiwasi Evam Baiga Vikas Utthan Samiti

The Trust AADIWASI EVAM BAIGA VIKAS UTTHAN SAMITI, DINDORI M.P was established by Mr. Kehar Singh Varma, Secretary of the trust. Looking at the current scenario and pandemic situation in our country, the president has decided to set up a hospital, so that the demand for the Hospital and doctors in the country will match with supply.

ABVUS promotes cleanliness and tribal culture, provides legal support to children, Beti Bachao Beti Padhao, labour registration camp, kanuni Sahayata camp, Cultural Rangoli and Nukkad Drama in schools and conducts women awareness camps to empower Dindori's vulnerable communities and ensure their holistic development.

ABVUS

Aadiwasi Evam Baiga Vikas Utthan Samiti is committed to empowering Dindori's vulnerable communities through holistic development initiatives in health, education, and cultural preservation.
